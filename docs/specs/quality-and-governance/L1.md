# Quality and governance subsystem — L1 requirements

## L1-QLT-001

- **Requirement:** Tranquility shall satisfy baseline platform, architecture-governance, and license-governance constraints.
- **Rationale:** Non-functional constraints are project-critical acceptance gates.
- **Source:** User brief §9 (2026-07-15)
- **Verification:** Inspection
- **Priority:** Must

## L1-QLT-002

- **Requirement:** Tranquility shall satisfy approved quantitative performance targets with documented verification evidence.
- **Rationale:** Performance must be contractual and testable rather than aspirational.
- **Source:** User brief §9 (2026-07-15), TBD-013
- **Verification:** Test
- **Priority:** Must
